Frequently Asked Questions

I can't seem to get Akhos, Cressidus or Patroka what am I doing wrong?

That would be because certain games are locked until you beat the game and start a new game plus play through. The blades unlocked in new game plus are Akhos, Cressidus, Obrona, Patroka and Perdido.

What is the best way to get legendary core crystals?

The Relentless Ardun superboss at the farm in Torigoth outside of the ardanian military base, is a very efficient way to farm legendary core crystals. In order to unlock the super boss you have to talk to the guy at the farm, and keep feeding the ardun until you can't anymore. It has a large health pool so you can smash it many times and has very low combo resistances. This can net you a lot of core crystals very fast.

What are the best blades to try and pull?

This question is kinda hard to answer. A lot of it is a matter of opinion. The main reason because the game provides you with the two best blades in the game. Which are Mythra and various forms of Poppi. Though KOS-MOS is one of the best blades in the base game. So trying to pull her is a very good idea. Another blade of note to pull is Adenine because she increases all drops and rewards from battle. All of the other blades are mostly equal.

I still have not gotten KOS-MOS what is up with that?

If you look at the table you will see that she has not only the lowest drop rate, she also never increases her drop rate no matter what group you are in. She is the rarest blade in the whole game so there are a couple of factors that can increase your chances at getting KOS-MOS. Considering she is a light blade that means she is affected by the justice driver stat. So make sure that the driver you are trying to pull her on has high justice. Also the driver has a stat called luck which has a couple of gameplay bonuses one of which it increases the rate at which rare blades drop so if you improve your luck stat on the selected driver it will greatly improve your chances at pulling KOS-MOS. The type of core crystal you use also greatly affects ones chances at pulling rare blades. Naturally the best variety of core crystal for pulling rare blades would be the legendary core crystal.
